Privacy notice

Aurora respects your privacy. This notice explains what we collect, how we use it, and how to exercise your rights under GDPR and CCPA.

What we collect

We collect the minimum information required to operate the service: account email, a hashed password, workspace metadata, and product telemetry that helps us debug crashes and improve UX flows.

How we use it

Strictly for delivering and improving the product. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for advertising. Aggregate, anonymized usage patterns may inform marketing copy.

Your rights

  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Request deletion (we honor within 30 days).
  • Opt out of analytics at any time from Settings → Privacy.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

Contact privacy@aurora.dev with questions.
