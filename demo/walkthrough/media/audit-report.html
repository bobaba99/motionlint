No prefers-reduced-motion path

🎯 Page stylesheet · (project-wide)

What's happeningThe page animates movement (transform / keyframes) but ships no `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` rule.
Why it mattersMotion-sensitive users get no relief; large movement can trigger nausea or vestibular symptoms.
FixAdd `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` that drops transform-based motion while keeping opacity/colour transitions.

Hover motion isn't gated for touch

🎯 Hover motion · (project-wide)

What's happeningHover-triggered motion isn't wrapped in `@media (hover: hover) and (pointer: fine)`.
Why it mattersTouch devices fire a synthetic hover on tap, so the animation plays on every touch — a false positive the user never intended.
FixGate hover animations behind `@media (hover: hover) and (pointer: fine)`.
